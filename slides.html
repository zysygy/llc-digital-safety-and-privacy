Notes

  • The last column is the maximum time it would take to crack a password. On average, it would take about half as much time and even less with more advanced logic.
  • The password processing method used in our calculations is called SHA-1, which is widely used. You don't need to know how it works, but here's an article with details.
  • Some websites will rate limit the number of tries you can make, but not all do. Hackers also commonly bypass this safeguard using an offline attack, which attempts to crack leaked password hashes.
